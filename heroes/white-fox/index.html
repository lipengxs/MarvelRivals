White Fox

STRATEGIST

Ami Han, the last of the legendary Kumiho, can summon her ancestral Nine-Tailed Power to fortify herself and heal her allies on the battlefield. As the calm and capable Director of Tiger Division, she is any team's unwavering backbone, no matter the foe.
