Start With Your Question

Use the page that matches the decision you are making. Hero profiles are a directory, while team guides explain a repeatable process rather than a single fixed meta lineup.

  • Find a hero and compare role labels before choosing a main or learning a matchup.
  • Understand Vanguard, Duelist, and Strategist jobs before copying a composition.
  • Build a team composition around engagement, protection, and reset plans.
  • Check Team-Up planning notes and confirm named interactions in the current game.
  • Read tracker data carefully without treating one patch or rank bracket as a universal answer.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the system requirements?

Minimum: Windows 10 64-bit, Intel i5/AMD Ryzen 5, 8GB RAM, NVIDIA GTX 1060/AMD RX 580

When is the release date?

Marvel Rivals launched on December 6, 2024 and is available now on PC and console storefronts.

Is the game free to play?

Yes! Marvel Rivals will be free to play with optional cosmetic purchases.

How many heroes are available at launch?

This site now indexes 51 heroes from the official hero listing, including post-launch roster additions through June 2026.

What game modes are available?

Marvel Rivals features various 6v6 game modes including Team Deathmatch, Objective Control, and special event modes. More modes will be added based on community feedback.

How do I join the beta test?

The beta period has passed. Use the download links above to install from Steam, Epic Games Store, PlayStation, or Xbox.

Will there be cross-platform play?

Cross-platform play is planned for future updates, allowing PC players to team up with console players.

How does the team composition work?

Teams consist of 6 players with different roles (Duelist, Vanguard, Strategist). Strategic hero combinations and team synergies are key to victory.
