Cyclops

DUELIST

Scott Summers leads the X-Men as Cyclops. Through his specialized ruby quartz visor, he precisely controls the devastating force channeled through eyes, neutralizing any threat on the battlefield. But when that visor comes off, all restraint disappears, erupting into an unstoppable torrent of concussive energy!
