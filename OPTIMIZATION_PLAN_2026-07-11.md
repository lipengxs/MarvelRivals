# Marvel Rivals SEO & content plan

## Baseline

GSC (2026-04-12 to 2026-07-11): 164 clicks, 12,773 impressions, 1.28% CTR, average position 22.48. `marvelrivals tracker` is the primary opportunity (5,748 impressions, position 7.11, 0.71% CTR).

## 0–30 days

- Make the tracker a genuinely useful, dated player tool; show its data source, coverage and update time.
- Clarify on every entry page that this is an independent fan guide and link to the official game site.
- Improve the tracker title, description and internal links from hero and team pages.
- Add `BreadcrumbList` and page-specific `FAQPage` markup only where visible FAQ content exists.

## 31–60 days

- Publish source-linked patch summaries and hero pages with role, counters, team synergies and version notes.
- Measure Steam/official-site outbound clicks and tracker interactions in GA4.

## 61–90 days and KPI

- Publish one verified patch or roster update within 24 hours of each official update; do not publish unverified roster claims.
- Target tracker CTR above 2%, tracker average position below 10, and at least 40% of organic clicks from non-tracker pages.
