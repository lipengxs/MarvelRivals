Devil Dinosaur

VANGUARD

Don't let his name scare you — Devil Dinosaur is actually a gentle giant. But with razor-sharp fangs, a mighty sweeping tail, a thunderous roar, and a force-field shield gifted by his human partner Moon Girl, it's no wonder why heroes and villains alike are intimidated by this prehistoric powerhouse!
